TelecomNet Product Catalog — Customer Service Reference

DATA BUNDLES
The 400MB hourly data bundle costs $1 and is valid for 1 hour. It is the cheapest available bundle suitable for quick email checks and light browsing.
The 1GB daily bundle costs $3 and is valid for 24 hours. This bundle covers typical daily social media and messaging use.
The 5GB weekly bundle costs $10 and is valid for 7 days. It is designed for moderate video streaming and remote work sessions throughout the week.
The 20GB monthly bundle costs $30 and is valid for 30 days. This provides the best per-megabyte value for heavy regular users.
The unlimited daily bundle costs $5 and provides unlimited data for 24 hours with a fair-use policy of 10GB at full LTE speed, after which speeds are reduced to 1 Mbps until midnight reset.
A family sharing plan allows up to 5 SIM cards to share a 50GB monthly pool for $60. Each SIM can be managed independently from the account dashboard.

VOICE CALLS
The standard local call rate is $0.10 per minute billed in 30-second increments. There is no connection fee.
International call rates start from $0.20 per minute to neighboring countries and up to $1.50 per minute for satellite-connected territories.
A 100-minute local call bundle costs $8 and is valid for 30 days. It cannot be used for international calls.
Wi-Fi calling is available on compatible handsets at standard local call rates. Enable it in your phone settings under Wi-Fi Calling or Calls over Wi-Fi.

SMS AND MESSAGING
Standard SMS rate is $0.05 per message to any local number.
The 100 SMS bundle costs $1 and is valid for 7 days.
The 500 SMS bundle costs $3 and is valid for 30 days.
MMS messages are billed at $0.15 each and require a data connection to be active.

ROAMING
International roaming is available in 50 countries. Activate the roaming add-on before departure via the MyTelecom app or by calling customer service at 100.
The daily roaming data pass costs $5 and gives 500MB at local network speeds.
Roaming voice calls are billed at $0.50 per minute outgoing and $0.25 per minute incoming.
Roaming SMS costs $0.10 per message sent. Receiving SMS while roaming is free.

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
Check your balance at any time by dialing star 123 hash, using the MyTelecom app, or visiting account.telecom.example.
Top-up is available via credit card, debit card, bank transfer, and voucher codes. The minimum top-up is $1 and the maximum single transaction is $500.
Enable auto-renew on any bundle in the MyTelecom app to avoid service interruptions. You will receive an SMS reminder 24 hours before renewal if your balance is insufficient.
Bundle activation takes effect immediately. A confirmation SMS is sent within 30 seconds of purchase.
Account transfers require visiting a store with government-issued photo identification and the current account holder present or a notarized letter of authorization.

BILLING AND PAYMENTS
Monthly billing statements are available in the MyTelecom app and by email. Paper statements can be requested for $1 per statement.
Payment is due within 15 days of the bill date. A $2 late fee applies after 15 days. Service may be suspended after 30 days of non-payment.
Disputed charges must be reported within 60 days of the bill date by calling 100 or submitting a ticket at support.telecom.example.
Direct debit can be set up from the billing section of the app to avoid missed payments.

DEVICE AND TECHNICAL SUPPORT
APN settings for Android devices: Name is TelecomNet, APN is internet.telecom, leave username and password blank, MCC is 310, MNC is 260.
APN settings for iPhone: navigate to Settings, then Cellular, then Cellular Data Network, and enter internet.telecom in the APN field.
If you experience no data connectivity, try toggling airplane mode off and on, or restart the device. If the problem persists, dial star 100 hash to reset network settings remotely.
Network coverage maps are available at coverage.telecom.example. Enter your address or postcode to check indoor and outdoor coverage strength.

SUPPORT CHANNELS
Customer service is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Call 100 from your TelecomNet number (free of charge) or plus 1 800 555 0100 from any phone.
Live chat is available in the MyTelecom app and at support.telecom.example during business hours 8am to 10pm local time.
Email support responds within 4 business hours at support@telecom.example.
Network outage information is posted in real time at status.telecom.example and on the official Twitter account at TelecomStatus.

SIM CARD SERVICES
SIM card replacement for lost, stolen, or damaged SIMs requires visiting a TelecomNet store with valid government-issued photo identification. The replacement fee is $2.
eSIM activation is available for compatible devices through the MyTelecom app. The process takes approximately 10 minutes.
SIM port-in from another carrier allows you to keep your existing number. Submit the port-in request online or in-store. The porting process completes within 24 hours after submission and requires your current carrier account number and PIN.
SIM swaps to a different device do not require a store visit if both devices support eSIM.
